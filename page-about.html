Hello, my name is Kirill.

I am BI developer and analyst. Being a big fan of visualization i try to explore tools like D3JS and custom vizializations in Power BI to bring complex data to visually pleasant and informative format. See my work on the start page by pressing HERE or by pressing HOME button on the left-side menu.

I ve gone a long way to switch to Analytics and BI development. Through those years i ve worked with many different tools like: Gooogle Bigquery, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, SQL DBs like MS SQL / PostgreSQL and data viz tools like Power BI, Google Datastudio, Tableau.

The final point was my decision to stay in BI developer role mainly and prioritize Power BI.

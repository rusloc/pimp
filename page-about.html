My field of interest lies in BI development and analytics. Being a big fan of visualization, I work with tools like D3JS and custom visualizations in Power BI to bring complex data to informative and visually pleasant form. You can check my projects on the start page by pressing HERE or by pressing HOME button in the menu.

I ve gone a long way to switch to Analytics and BI development. Through those years i ve worked with many different tools like: Gooogle Bigquery, Google Analytics, Google Tag Manager, SQL DBs like MS SQL / PostgreSQL and data viz tools like Power BI, Google Datastudio, Tableau. The final point was my decision to stay in BI developer role mainly and prioritize Power BI.

While working on projects and for companies i dealt with report building tasks in areas such as Management, Marketing, Operations, Sales, Customer service and others.
